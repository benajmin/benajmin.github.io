The Average Bill

November 2017

After reading Craig Kaplan's blog post about producing an amalgamation using canadain currency, I decided to do the same with the new plastic bills. Luckily the Bank of Canada provides images of all the bills on their website, so I was easily able to take the average image of both the front and back sides.

Sadly, the provided images were all watermarked, but I feel the averaged image still clearly shows the similar structure between the bills

It's also interesting to note that by doing this we also get the average face of many Canadian historical figured
